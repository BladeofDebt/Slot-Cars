
2 Player PVP.
Bullet bends around turns in the way you are aiming when fired.
Modeled after Atari 2600's Slot Racers.

Controls:
Player 1:
W		Aim Forwards
A		Aim Left
S		Brake
D		Aim Right
F		Boost
G		Fire

Player 2:
Up		Aim Forwards
Left		Aim Left
Down		Brake
Right		Aim Right
Keypad 0	Boost
Keypad DEL	Fire
